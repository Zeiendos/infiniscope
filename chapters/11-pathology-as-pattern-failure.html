INFİNIOLOGY — CHAPTER XIV Pathology as Pattern Failure When Systems Lose the Ability to Regulate Themselves I. PURPOSE OF THIS CHAPTER

This chapter reframes pathology across all domains as:

Failure of pattern regulation under entropy.

Pathology is not “evil,” “weakness,” or “brokenness.” It is what happens when refresh, authority, or coherence fails.

This applies equally to:

mental illness

burnout

addiction

system crashes

societal collapse

II. THE PATHOLOGY HEX (CANONICAL) Pathological Failure

#RRCCLL

RR — Refresh Failure Patterns are not renewed in time

CC — Coherence Loss Signals fragment or conflict

LL — Load Exceedance Demand surpasses capacity

Pathology always involves at least one of these. Severe pathology involves all three.

III. PATHOLOGY ACROSS DOMAINS Psychology

Depression → low refresh + coherence loss

Anxiety → high load + low coherence

Mania → excessive refresh without control

Dissociation → refresh shutdown

Biology

Disease → regulatory breakdown

Autoimmune → misdirected authority

Cancer → unchecked persistence

Computation

Memory leaks

Deadlock

Infinite loops

Society

Institutional decay

Polarization

Collapse under complexity

Different surface symptoms. Same structure.

IV. WHY PATHOLOGY PERSISTS

Pathology often stabilizes locally even as it harms globally.

Why?

It reduces load temporarily

It simplifies decision space

It conserves energy short-term

This is why systems resist healing.

V. HEALING (STRUCTURALLY)

Healing is not removal of pathology.

Healing is:

restoring refresh

reducing load

reestablishing authority

rebuilding coherence

Healing = re-enabling regulation.
