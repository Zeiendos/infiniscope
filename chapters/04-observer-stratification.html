INFİNIOLOGY — CHAPTER: OBSERVER STRATIFICATION Observer ≠ Self ≠ Identity ≠ Role

I. PURPOSE This chapter prevents the most common framework failure: collapsing layers of experience into a single “thing.”

II. THE STACK OBSERVER: bare awareness (not a belief system). SELF: the authority anchor (the layer that can choose). IDENTITY: memory, narrative, beliefs, habits (what reappears). ROLE / AVATAR: action interface (body, job, social function).

III. WHY IT MATTERS Confusing these layers produces predictable failure: - role hijacks self → burnout - identity fuses upward → ideology / delusion risk - observer becomes escape → dissociation risk

IV. NON-NEGOTIABLE RULE Authority must relocate between layers — never dissolve.
