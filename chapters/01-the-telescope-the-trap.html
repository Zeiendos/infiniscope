INFİNIOLOGY — THE TELESCOPE & THE TRAP Why frameworks fail, and how to keep this one clean

I. THE TELESCOPE Stars = phenomena. Lenses = fields of study. Alignment = coherence. Zoom = load. Image hold = persistence. Builder = lens architect. Observer = practitioner.

II. THE TRAP (COMMON MISUSE) Frameworks become dangerous when: - symbols are literalized - authority is outsourced to the model - belief becomes exemption from consequence - the operator confuses identity with the tool

III. SAFETY RULES (LOCK THESE) - Use the lens. Don’t become it. - Belief ≠ exemption from consequence. - Operator ≠ system. - If authority dissolves, stop and rebind to role layer.

IV. WHY THIS MATTERS A good telescope shows more detail. A bad user thinks the telescope is the sky.
