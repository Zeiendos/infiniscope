INFİNIOLOGY — CHAPTER: SOMATIC FLOW SYSTEMS (NON-LITERAL) Qi • Meridians • Chakras as Regulation Maps

I. PURPOSE This chapter explains why somatic “energy” models can be useful without being literal physics.

II. CANON TRANSLATION “Energy” = felt functional flow (coordination + circulation + attention + emotion), not a physical force.

III. WHY THESE MODELS WORK (WHEN USED CLEANLY) - correct resolution (body-scale regulation, not particle-scale claims) - pattern-based tracking (tension, breath, heat, fatigue, calm) - feedback loops (you can feel changes) - balance framing (downshift vs upshift)

IV. FAILURE MODES (DO NOT DO THIS) - literalization (“I have blocked energy as a substance”) - authority transfer (“the model knows better than reality”) - medical replacement (substituting symbols for care) - belief absolutism (treating metaphors as laws)

V. PRACTICAL USE Use somatic models as *interfaces* to regulate load, coherence, and persistence in the body: - downshift arousal (breath + pace) - restore coherence (posture + attention) - protect persistence (sleep + recovery)

VI. ANCHOR SENTENCE Symbols can guide regulation; they do not override responsibility.
